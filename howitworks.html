Application Process

1. Submit Application

Fill out our straightforward online application with information about yourself, your creative work, and how you plan to use the funds.

2. Credit Check

We perform a soft credit check (which doesn't affect your score) to evaluate your eligibility.

3. Application Review

Our team reviews your application, considering your credit history, creative portfolio, and business plan.

4. Get Approved

Upon approval, you'll receive your loan offer with clear terms and repayment schedule.

5. Receive Funding

After accepting the terms, funds are transferred directly to your account, typically within 24-48 hours.

6. Join Our Community

Gain access to our community of creators, resources, and events to help your creative career flourish.
